Our Pillars

For our 5th edition, we decided to change the rules a little bit and use this Weekly Dose as a mean to share our standing pillars.

We praise projects that are radically honest, and we wanted to make sure that you get access to the underlying thinking and foundation behind what is to come. Enjoy!



At human.space, we’re fighting a peaceful war against lost potential.

As humans, we believe it’s our right to forge our own path in life & our duty to work for it. And that even though it's going to be rough sometimes, it's our responsibility to keep pushing.

We're all in this together & human.space is there for you every step of the way. On this positive note, let's introduce you to the 3 commandments human.space stands for.

#1 - Master Your Smile: Find Your Path

In this human space, we're all about self-discovery, digging into our subconscious mind, learning & growing. We respect this sacred process more than anything else, so we might live the deepest human experience possible and connect to our life’s path.

We have a compass which can guide us to that life path. It’s an inner voice, which itself has many forms: Intuitions, inclinations, visions & dreams — but most importantly:

The Smile Of Life.

The Smile of Life is an instinctive experience of personal truth. Do whatever you want with it, but once you touch it, going back is going against yourself.

That is the human.space currency. We share Smiles of Life.

#2 - Master yourself: The Mental Game

Here’s the thing: following Smiles of Life is a quest filled with inner obstacles.

This beautiful quest forces us to chase limiting beliefs from our minds, tame our egos, conquer our fears, remove doubts, procrastination & negativity. Doing that mental work allows us to foster inner peace & harmony with the world, which represents an integral part of our holistic view of the human life.

#3 — Master Your Craft: The Skill Game

We seek mastery as a way to move the world forward. Skill gives weight to our dreams, materializing our visions into the real world, where it can have an impact on generations to come.

We believe that the Renaissance ideal of the Universal Human is not dead. We believe in mastering many fields. Connecting the dots is the way of the future. We will push our creations further through respect for the craft.

We will seek to innovate, never settling for the status quo.

Your Next Step

All right, if you're reading this, then you understand that we're all about going deeper into human life & pushing away self-imposed limits :)

The ultimate goal is for you to learn from our mistakes & successes, and use the valuable insights while to pursue YOUR Smiles of Life.



Hence, let us close our 5th edition with a question:


What do you truly stand for? |or| What are your guiding principles?


If one (or five!) of your best friends are also on the path to become their best selves: Send them this weekly dose, they'll be glad you did!

Have a wonderful day,

With love, from the human.space team
